Privacy Policy — QHN Pediatrics On-Call Tool

Last updated: July 2026

This tool is an internal scheduling application used by the QHN Pediatrics residency program to manage and communicate the on-call duty rota. It includes a WhatsApp assistant that lets residents ask about the schedule and lets chief residents update it.

What information we collect

  • Resident names, training level, rotation, and contact information (phone number) already maintained by the program for scheduling purposes.
  • The on-call schedule itself (who is assigned to which call slot, on which date).
  • The content of messages sent to the WhatsApp assistant, in order to answer schedule questions or process authorized schedule changes.

How we use it

  • To identify who is messaging the assistant, using the sender's phone number matched against the resident directory.
  • To answer questions about the on-call schedule.
  • To allow designated chief residents to review and confirm schedule changes.
  • To maintain an audit log of schedule changes made via WhatsApp, for accountability.

Where data is stored

Schedule and resident data is stored using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), operated on Google Cloud infrastructure. Message text is processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses, and delivered via Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform. Data is not sold, and is not used for advertising.

Who can access data

The schedule is viewable by anyone with the program's link (matching how the schedule has always been shared). Schedule edits require chief-resident authorization. Only designated administrators can modify resident records.

Contact

Questions about this tool or your data can be directed to the program's chief resident team.
